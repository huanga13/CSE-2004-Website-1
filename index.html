DIM SUM

char siu bao

Char siu is Cantonese-style barbecued pork marinated in a sweet and savory sauce of soy, hoisin, honey, and five-spice. It is roasted until glossy and slightly caramelized at the edges. The result is tender meat with a signature reddish glaze.

har gow

Har gow are Cantonese dumplings filled with plump, juicy shrimp. Their translucent, slightly chewy wrappers are pleated by hand and steamed until tender.

xiao long bao

Xiao long bao are thin-skinned dumplings filled with seasoned pork and rich, savory broth. When steamed, the gelatinized stock melts into soup inside the wrapper.
